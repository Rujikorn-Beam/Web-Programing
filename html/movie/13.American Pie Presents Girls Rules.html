American Pie Presents: Girls' Rules (2020)

In this spin-off of the American Pie franchise, the girls take over the quest for love. Looking to spice up their love lives before graduation, four female high school seniors band together to harness their girl power and get what they want from the boys.

Director: Mike Elliott
Writers: Adam Herz (based on characters created by), Blayne Weaver
Stars: Madison Pettis, Lizze Broadway, Natasha Behnam
